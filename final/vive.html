Vive - under construction

This project is a continuation of the "100 holocaust survivors" an organized collection of data. After uncovering what my data represents and my findings, I decided to expand the concept further more and design and prototype an app to allow the user explore my database interactively with even more content, any survivor.

Bringing together concepts from information architecture, visual design, and interaction de- sign, the eld of User Interface [UI] Design (or User Experience [UX] Design) focuses on what users might need to do, feel, and know, while interacting with a product and ensuring that the interface’s functional and visual elements facilitate those actions, provide access to informa- tion and therefore understanding.
